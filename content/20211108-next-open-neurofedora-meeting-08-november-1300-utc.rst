Please join us at the next regular Open NeuroFedora team meeting on Monday 08 November at 1300UTC in #fedora-neuro on IRC (Libera.chat).
The meeting is a public meeting, and open for everyone to attend.
You can join us over:

- `Matrix <https://matrix.to/#/%23neuro:fedoraproject.org>`__
- `IRC <https://webchat.libera.chat/?channels=#fedora-neuro>`__

You can use `this link <https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?msg=NeuroFedora+Meeting&iso=20211108T13&p1=1440&ah=1>`__ to convert the meeting time to your local time.
Or, you can also use this command in the terminal:

.. code-block:: bash

    $ date --date='TZ="UTC" 1300 2021-11-08'


The meeting will be chaired by `@oluyosola`.
The agenda for the meeting is:

- New introductions and roll call.
- `Tasks from last meeting <https://meetbot.fedoraproject.org/teams/neurofedora/neurofedora.2021-10-25-13.01.html>`__.
- `Open Pagure tickets <https://pagure.io/neuro-sig/NeuroFedora/issues?status=Open&tags=S%3A+Next+meeting>`__.
- `Package health check <https://packager-dashboard.fedoraproject.org/neuro-sig>`__.
- `Open package reviews check <https://bugzilla.redhat.com/show_bug.cgi?id=fedora-neuro>`__.
- `CompNeuro lab compose status check for Fedora 36 <https://koji.fedoraproject.org/koji/packageinfo?packageID=30691>`__.
- Neuroscience query of the week
- Next meeting day, and chair.
- Open floor.

We hope to see you there!
